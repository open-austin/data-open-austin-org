In this directory, we publish a LIVES data feed, updated nightly,
with restaurant inspection scores from the Austin/Travis County
Health Department.

The LIVES data format is described here:

http://www.yelp.com/healthscores

More info here:

http://www.codeforamerica.org/blog/2013/01/17/foodies-and-open-data-enthusiasts-rejoice/

The feed generator is open source, available here:

https://github.com/chip-rosenthal/atx-lives-feed
